Originally from France, Rudy Roots is a versatile DJ, artist, composer, producer, and event organizer who has successfully expanded his influence internationally, particularly in India. For several years, he has been traveling regularly to Goa, a destination famous for its beaches and vibrant music scene. In Goa, Rudy Roots not only organizes events like the renowned "Reggae Rules The Beach" parties but also performs as a DJ, captivating audiences with his sets.

He contributes to Goa Sunsplash, a well-known reggae festival that draws artists and reggae enthusiasts from around the globe. Recently, Rudy Roots has begun releasing his first tracks, collaborating with various international singers. Through his music and events, he continues to promote reggae culture, forging cultural connections between France and India.
